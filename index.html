HOW TO AVOID SCAMMERS ONLINE


by Heart Montero and Marcus Baraniano

These days, the number of scammers on the internet increases. Many people especially the elderly people have been experiencing online scams. Because of this it causes trouble for people to use the internet. Here are some ways to prevent and avoid online scams and scammers on the internet:

1. Be cautious about requested messages about your personal information.

2. Always DO your research before entering websites.

3. Never share your personal information online.

4. Do update your Security Software to detect and prevent scams.

5. Report online scam activities to the authorities.

Beware of Online Scams to Prevent them!
